At StreetLyfe, we bring the bold and vibrant flavors of street food to your plate with a modern twist. Inspired by India’s rich street food culture, our menu is a celebration of local favorites like fried rice, noodles, and refreshing mojitos — all made fresh, fast, and full of flavor.
